YOUR CLIENTS
NEVER
NEED AN
APP.

95% of coach–client communication in India happens on WhatsApp. Fitosys works inside the channel your clients already use. No downloads. No logins. No friction.

Messages Come From Your Number
Every automated message is sent from your registered WhatsApp business number — in your name. To your clients, it looks like you sent it personally.
Approved Message Templates
All WhatsApp messages use Meta-approved templates. Fully compliant with WhatsApp Business Policy. No risk of account restrictions.

WhatsApp Business API charges ₹1.09 per marketing message. For 30 clients, monthly messaging cost is under ₹300. Fitosys passes this through at cost — no markup.
