Section 04
CTA
Alignment

Why "Get Started" and "Request a Demo" are sending different signals — and what to replace them with.

❌ Current — Misaligned
Nav: "Get Started"  |  Footer: "Request a Demo"
These are two different buyer intents on the same product. "Get Started" implies you can sign up immediately. "Request a Demo" implies the product is complex, expensive, or needs a sales call. For independent fitness coaches — your actual audience — a demo request is a conversion killer. It adds friction and signals enterprise pricing. They'll leave before filling the form.
✅ Recommended — Unified
Nav: "Start Free"  |  Footer: "Start Free"
Same intent, same action, same page. Every CTA goes to /signup. "Free" removes the cost fear. "Start" creates momentum. The footer can have a supporting line: "No card needed · First 5 clients free." This aligns with your Starter tier freemium model and removes all ambiguity about what happens when they click.
